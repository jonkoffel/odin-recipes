Gado Gado

Description

An Indonesian staple and easy to make than it looks.

Ingredients

  • Peanuts
  • Galangal or ginger
  • Rice noodles
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook rice noodles until al dente
  2. Crush peanuts and galangal in dish to create a paste
  3. Combine cooked noodles and savory mixture

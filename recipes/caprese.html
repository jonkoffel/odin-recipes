Caprese Salad

Description

A refreshing warm weather meal for when you cannot stand to turn on the stove. Best with crusty bread on the side.

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Heirloom tomatoes
  • Basil, torn into small pieces
  • Balsamic vinegar for drizzling

Steps

  1. Slice the mozzarella into small rounds or tear into grape-sized pieces
  2. Slice the tomato into rounds, does not matter if large
  3. Layer the tomatoes and cheese on each plate
  4. Top with torn basil and a drizzle of balsamic
